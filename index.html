When your run involves splashing through slush, you need a little extra protection against the mess. Our Thaw Merino pieces combine the warmth and wicking properties of our Harrier wool blend with an innovative eco finish that keeps you drier, longer – no matter how many puddles you encounter.

The water and stain repellent HeiQ eco-dry finish repels water and stains, preventing dirt from bonding to the fabric. It's also PFC-free, which helps keep harmful chemicals out of the snow, ice and water.
Merino wool is naturally thermo-regulating, which means it keeps you warm when it’s cold and cool as things heat up. It also dries in a snap. Lanolin in the Merino yarns prevents bacteria from colonizing, preventing the dreaded sweat-stink.
In Vermont, they say there are five seasons: spring, summer, fall, winter and mud – when thawing snowmelt turns dirt roads and trails into a soggy mess. While it may not have achieved “season status” everywhere, that wet, mushy period between deep winter and early spring is a fact of life for many runners. We spend it leaping over slush puddles in old trainers that we don’t mind getting really dirty. It’s the physical in-between. It’s the thaw. Inspired by this glorious messy season, we updated our training staples to handle some serious splashes.
